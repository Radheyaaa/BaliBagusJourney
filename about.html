Our Story

From Local Boy to
Bali's Top Guide

I'm Wayan — a Balinese name meaning "first-born." Born in Ubud, I grew up climbing the terraces of Tegallalang, swimming at hidden waterfalls, and watching the sun set over Tanah Lot with my family.

What started as helping a few backpackers find their way around in 2016 turned into a full-time passion. I realized tourists didn't just want a driver — they wanted a friend who knew Bali inside out.

Today, Bali Bagus Journey serves travelers from over 40 countries. My mission is simple: to share the Bali I grew up in — the real one, not just the tourist trail — with every person who steps into my car.

Our Journey

Building Trust, One Tour at a Time

2016 — The Beginning

Started as a Solo Driver

Began with a single car and a passion for sharing Bali with the world. First 50 tourists led to 5-star reviews and word-of-mouth growth.

2018 — Growing the Team

Added Island Tours & Boat Trips

Expanded service to include Nusa Penida and Nusa Lembongan. Partnered with trusted local boat operators for seamless island experiences.

2020 — Adaptation

Safety-First Protocols

Navigated through challenging times while maintaining safe, clean vehicles and eventually welcoming travelers back with enhanced safety measures.

2022–Present — Flourishing

2,000+ Happy Tourists & Counting

Now offering 15+ curated packages, serving guests from 40+ countries, and consistently maintaining a 4.9-star rating across all platforms.
